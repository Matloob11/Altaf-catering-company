Frequently Asked Questions

You can visit us at MM Farm House Sharif Medical Jati Umrah Road, Karachi. For consultations, you can either call us at +923039907296 or book an appointment through our website. We recommend scheduling appointments in advance, especially during peak wedding seasons. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.

We provide catering services throughout major cities in Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and surrounding areas. We can also cater to other cities with additional transportation and logistics arrangements. Please contact us to discuss specific location requirements.

For consultation meetings, please bring: 1) Event details (date, time, venue), 2) Approximate guest count, 3) Any dietary restrictions or preferences, 4) Your budget range, 5) Any specific menu items you'd like to include. Having these details helps us provide accurate quotes and suggestions.

Booking is simple: 1) Fill out our online booking form or call us directly, 2) Schedule a consultation meeting, 3) Choose your menu and services, 4) Pay the booking deposit to secure your date. We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for large events.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cash. For events, we typically require a 50% advance payment to confirm the booking, with the remaining balance due one week before the event date. All payments are secured with proper documentation.
