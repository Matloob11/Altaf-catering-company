5 Tips to Keep Food Fresh at Large Events

10 Oct 2025 • By Chef Altaf • 4 min read

Keeping food fresh across long events is a challenge; with the right planning you can preserve flavor, texture and appearance. Here are five practical tips our chefs use every day:

  • Stagger service and use warming stations to avoid buffet food sitting out too long.
  • Hold sauces and dressings separately and add them just before serving.
  • Use insulated chafing and proper lids to retain heat without drying food.
  • Keep cold items on ice and replenish frequently to maintain safe temperatures.
  • Train staff to rotate and monitor dishes with a simple checklist.

Follow these and your guests will always get the freshest experience from your catering.

How to Choose the Right Catering Package

22 Aug 2025 • By Omar Khan • 3 min read

Choosing a catering package depends on event type, guest count, and service level. Before booking, decide whether you need plated service, buffet or finger food. Consider the following:

  • Budget per person vs total headcount.
  • Service style — plated gives more control; buffet is cost effective for large groups.
  • Dietary restrictions and how many options you need.

Contact us with your date and guest estimate and we’ll recommend the best package.
