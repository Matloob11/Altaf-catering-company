Our Privacy Policy

Please read how we handle and protect your information

Below is a clear summary of how Altaf Catering collects, uses, protects, and shares personal information — presented in focused sections for quick scanning.

Scope & Effective Date

This policy applies to information collected through our website and related services. Effective date: Immediately. We may update this policy; updated versions will include the effective date.

Information We Collect

Examples: contact details, booking details, payment transaction metadata, and communication content you provide. We may also collect device and analytics data automatically (IP address, browser, pages visited).

How We Use Information

To provide and improve services, process bookings/payments, send confirmations and marketing (with consent), and detect fraud. We use data to tailor the experience and to comply with legal obligations.

How We Share Information

We share data with trusted service providers (payment processors, email delivery services, analytics). We do not sell personal data. Where required by law, we may disclose data to authorities.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes it was collected, or as required by law. Retention periods vary by data type.

Security & Breach Response

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. In the unlikely event of a breach, we'll follow legal notification requirements and notify affected users where required.

Your Rights & Choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data. You can opt out of marketing communications via the unsubscribe link in emails or by contacting us directly.

Third Parties & International Transfers

Some providers may process data outside your country. We take contractual and technical steps to protect data transferred internationally.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences at any time. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us data, please contact us to have it removed.

Social Media & Public Interaction

If you interact with us through social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram), please note that your interactions and data are also subject to those platforms’ privacy policies. We encourage you to review them for more details.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such third-party sites. Please review their policies before submitting any personal information.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through our website or via email.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage visitors to check this page periodically.

Email & Communication Policy

We may send you service updates, booking confirmations, or promotional messages. You can unsubscribe anytime. We do not share your email address with advertisers or external marketers without your consent.

Data Accuracy & Updates

We rely on users to provide accurate personal data. You may request corrections if your details are outdated or incorrect, and we will update them promptly.

Event & Catering Booking Data

When you book an event with us, we collect details such as menu preferences, event date, and location. This data is used solely to manage your booking and ensure smooth service delivery.

Payment Information Handling

Payments are securely processed through third-party gateways. We never store your full credit card information. All transactions are encrypted and compliant with PCI standards.

Data Access by Employees

Only authorized staff members who need access to personal data for legitimate business purposes (like processing bookings) can view it. All employees follow strict confidentiality obligations.

Legal Compliance & Enforcement

We may disclose data if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from public authorities. We will take all reasonable steps to ensure your rights are protected in such cases.

Contact & Updates

Questions or requests: altafcatering@gmail.com. For booking-related queries use our Contact page.

Notes:

  • We recommend reviewing this page periodically for updates.
  • If you need a copy of your data or wish to exercise your rights, contact us.
